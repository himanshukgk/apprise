Happy Monsoon

Issue: 28 | July, 2021

Dear KGKians,

I hope you all are abiding by the necessary precautions and contributing your best to health, family and work.
Today, I would like to share with you all is “How to Start &Stay Strong on a Long-Term Project”. More than often when encountered with a new project, we tend to jump towards it. However, to make sure you not only start strong but stay strong on a long-term project, set upper and lower bounds for how much time you’ll dedicate to it.
My dears, staying within those boundaries will help you defend against your own bad tendencies whether that's procrastinating for days on end, only to cram in a lot of time at the finish line, or overextending yourself early on to the point.
Therefore, work on your schedule, plan the day, and do not put your task on hold for the next day, which you can easily manage today.

-Navrattan Kothari
